This exquisite tea basket represents the pinnacle of traditional bamboo weaving craftsmanship. Handcrafted by Master Chen, a third-generation bamboo artisan from Sichuan province, each basket takes over 40 hours to complete.

The intricate cross-weaving pattern creates both beauty and functionality, with the tight weave ensuring durability while maintaining the basket's lightweight nature. The natural bamboo material has been carefully selected and treated to enhance its longevity while preserving its authentic character.

Perfect for storing and presenting your finest tea collections, this basket features a removable inner compartment with three sections, allowing you to organize different tea varieties. The natural antibacterial properties of bamboo help maintain the freshness of your tea leaves.

Artisan Story

Master Chen learned bamboo weaving from his grandfather at the age of seven. His work has been recognized nationally, with pieces displayed in the National Museum of Chinese Arts and Crafts. Each basket carries his signature weaving technique that combines traditional methods with subtle contemporary elements.

Dimensions

  • Diameter 22 cm (8.7 inches)

Materials

  • Primary Natural Moso Bamboo

Care Instructions

  • Clean with a slightly damp cloth. Do not submerge in water.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources to prevent drying and cracking.
  • Apply food-safe bamboo oil every 6 months to maintain flexibility and luster.

Origin

Handcrafted in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, China
